About Me

Welcome to my website. My name is Ryan Evans, I have a B.A. in Kinesiology with and emphasis in physical therapy from Cal State Dominguez Hills. While at Dominguez Hills, I played for the univsities Soccer team, winning multiple CCAA championships and a National Title in 2008. I have now changed my focus from solving problems on the soccer field to solving problems on the internet.

After graduating from Dominguez hills, I worked many different fields for Distribution company, Operation managment for a packaging plant, costumer service, outside sales, and regulatory compliance. After alot of thought I begin my journey to break into the tech field as I feel I can make a bigger impact helping solve difficult problems and push my self to learn and enjoy working with a team again. Take a look around and hopefully we will get a chance to work together in the future. Have a good day!
