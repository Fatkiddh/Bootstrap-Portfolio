My name is Ryan Evans, I have a Bachelors in Kinesiology with and emphasis in physical therapy from Cal State Dominguez Hills. While at Dominguez Hills, I played for the univsities Soccer team, winning multiple CCAA championships and a National Title in 2008. I have now changed my focus from solving problems on the soccer field to solving problems on the internet.

After graduating from Dominguez hills, I worked many different fields for Distribution a company, Operation managment for a packaging plant, costumer service, outside sales, and regulatory/compliance. After alot of thought I begin my journey to break into the tech field as I feel I can make a bigger impact helping solve difficult problems and push my self to learn and enjoy working with a team again.

Some of the things I enjoy in my free time include: playing soccer, takes up about all my free time but I love it. Being from Huntington Beach, I think it is a given I love the beach and on most sunny days I have free this is where you will find me. I also enjoy camping, bbq, traveling.
